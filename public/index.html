What MEMBRA can price

  • GitHub repositories and software prototypes
  • Websites, domains, SEO assets, and newsletters
  • Datasets, AI models, prompts, PDFs, books, courses
  • Creator accounts and social attention assets
  • Ecommerce and marketplace listings

Compliance-first boundary

  • Verified ownership before serious appraisal
  • API/OAuth evidence beats screenshots
  • Platform transferability must be documented
  • Private data, credentials, stolen assets, and fake traffic are blocked
  • Pending state is evidence, not settlement
