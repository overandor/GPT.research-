## Evidence Quality Levels

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Level 0 | Narrative only |
| Level 1 | Screenshot or manual claim |
| Level 2 | Repository path or code artifact |
| Level 3 | Test/log evidence |
| Level 4 | Reviewed by third party or buyer |
| Level 5 | Transaction-backed or audit-confirmed |

## Dossier Rule

The appraisal should remain labeled **provisional** until at least Level 3 evidence exists for the core control-plane claims and Level 4 or Level 5 evidence exists for market or institutional value.
